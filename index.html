About Me

Welcome to my corner of the internet! This website is my virtual campfire where I showcase my skills, passions, and the concoction of code and creativity that makes me who I am. Grab a virtual marshmallow, and let's dive into this expedition!

  • Name: Nick Cook
  • Age: 33

Ah, let me regale you with the tale of the Montana native turned digital nomad! Meet the enigmatic tech-sorcerer known as Nicholas C. Cook, hailing from the vast and wild landscapes of Montana. From the very beginning, curiosity ran through his veins like a river through the Rockies. His journey into the world of tinkering and tech started with a bang, quite literally, as he tore things apart with youthful abandon. At first, it seemed like nothing would ever be whole again, but a remarkable transformation was underway. Gradually, the chaos transformed into mastery. Like a phoenix rising from the ashes, he discovered his knack for repairing the very contraptions he had once dismantled with wild enthusiasm. It was a revelation, a testament to his innate understanding of how things worked in this intricate techno-verse. At the tender age of 21, our adventurer embarked on a quest of a different kind. He ventured westward to the rugged lands of northern California, where the grid was but a distant memory. Off the grid living became his way of life, a digital hermitage amidst the redwoods and rivers. Here, he learned to harness nature's gifts while carving out a unique connection to the world, one devoid of screens and city lights. But, as the saying goes, all paths lead back home. Nick has now returned to the welcoming embrace of Montana, his birthplace and the landscape that has always fueled his adventurous spirit. With a heart still touched by the wilderness and a mind ignited by the endless possibilities of technology, he is on the verge of a grand transformation. The goal? To pivot his career towards the mesmerizing world of computer science, where he'll blend his love for the great outdoors with his passion for coding, creating a unique synergy that promises to reshape the digital wilderness.
